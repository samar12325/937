✅ How Department Filtering Works

The department dropdown now properly filters the chart. Here's how to test it:

Step 1: Open the main report page
Step 2: Click on the department dropdown (اختر الإدارة/القسم)
Step 3: Select any department from the list
Step 4: The chart will show only that department's data
Step 5: Select "الكل" (All) to show all departments again

📊 What You Should See

  • Chart Title Changes: When filtering, the title becomes "البلاغات في: [Department Name]"
  • Title Color: The title turns green when filtering is active
  • Single Bar: Only one department bar appears when filtering
  • All Bars: All 70 departments appear when "الكل" is selected
